## 💡 TIPS FOR SENDING

### Best Practices:
1. **Timing**: Send on weekday between 9-11 AM or 2-4 PM
2. **Follow-up**: If no response in 48 hours, send one follow-up message
3. **Personalization**: Replace with actual hackathon name if you have it
4. **Contact**: Get the hackathon organizer's WhatsApp directly (check hackathon website/social media)

### What to Include:
- ✅ Your name + title
- ✅ Company name (XtraSecurity)
- ✅ Website (xtrasecurity.in)
- ✅ Offer clearly stated
- ✅ Call to action

### What NOT to Do:
- ❌ Don't be too salesy
- ❌ Don't make it too long (WhatsApp users skim)
- ❌ Don't use too many emojis (looks unprofessional)
- ❌ Don't ask for anything immediately
